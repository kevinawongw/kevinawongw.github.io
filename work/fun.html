meet kevina — when she's not working.

When I am not programming or studying you can find me at your local coffee shop or restaurant. I love food and discovering new kinds of foods. Because of my love of food, I have also picked up baking, cooking, and food photography. I used to be a barista in high school, and my love for making drinks has never left, so every morning, you will definitely see me carrying a latté that I probably made.

I love to travel as well. I have been fortunate to be able to travel to Asian countries like Japan, Vietnam, and Hong Kong several of times with my family. Those experiences have made me fall in love with immersing myself in new cultures. I was also able to travel to China for a study-abroad program where I was able to learn about it's deep-rooted history. These experiences have inspired me to learn new languages, so when I'm not studying (about computer science), you might still find me studying, but about languages this time! I've taken on learning Mandarin and Japanese, and I have a lot of fun learning languages.
